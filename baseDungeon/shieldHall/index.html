Shield Hall (Z10)

Shield Hall
A small stair leads from a landing to another floor just below it. Arms and armor are mounted on the walls. All are rusted and ruined, save for a round shield, painted red with two battered longswords hanging behind it.


Behind the shield are are two rusty flying swords that attack any creature that removes the shield from the wall. The two levels of this chamber are 10 feet apart and connected by the stairs. Treasure. The shield on the wall is a shield of missile attraction that appears to be an arrowcatching shield.


Dungeon Flow
  • If the players don't interact with the shield, they can continue onto Z11, AKA the Water Trap Room.
